🎶 TypingTunes – Improve Typing Speed with Song Lyrics! 🎹
TypingTunes is a unique typing practice tool that lets you improve your typing speed and accuracy by typing song lyrics in real time. Choose your speed level, upload your favorite lyrics, and challenge yourself while tracking your WPM (words per minute) and accuracy live.

✨ Features
✅ Upload Any Lyrics – Copy & paste your favorite song lyrics and start typing!
✅ Live Accuracy & WPM Tracking – See how fast and accurately you type.
✅ Dynamic Lyrics Display – Only four lines at a time for better focus and readability.
✅ Instant Feedback – Correct words turn blue, mistakes turn red.
✅ Multiple Speed Modes – Choose from Normal, Fast, or Insane speeds for a challenge.
✅ No Login Required – Simple and fast, fully client-side.

🚀 How to Use
Copy & paste song lyrics into the input box.
Click "Load Lyrics" to start the typing test.
Type along while the lyrics are displayed.
Get real-time feedback on accuracy and speed.
Complete the lyrics and see your final typing stats!
🛠 Tech Stack
HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
Fully client-side, lightweight & fast
